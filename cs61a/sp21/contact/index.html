Contact Us

There are several ways to contact course staff:

  • Private Piazza post: These can be seen by all course staff (instructors, TAs, tutors). Please do not direct posts to individual staff members, as they're likely to be missed. If you're asking a question about your assignment code, please follow the private post template.
  • cs61a@berkeley.edu: Messages sent to this email can be seen by this semester's TAs and instructors. We keep the emails, so future TAs will also have access. We can delete any email you sent us after the fact if you request it. If you are sharing personal information, you may not want to send it there.
  • OH feedback form: This is an anonymous Google form that is readable only by the instructors and is specifically for giving feedback on interactions you've had with course staff during office hours (negative or positive!).
  • General feedback form: This is another anonymous Google form that is readable only by the instructors. Please use it to share any feedback about the course and how we can improve it.
  • Extension form: Please use this form if requesting a deadline extension. This is viewable only by the head TAs of this semester.
  • Individual email addresses: All of our email addresses are listed on the staff pages. We encourage you to use the options above if they meet your needs, since they will reach more people. However, this is a good option when sharing personal information.

The EECS department also provides many ways to report issues within the department. Please do report any incidents to us or the department, so that we can continue to improve the CS student experience. Thank you!
