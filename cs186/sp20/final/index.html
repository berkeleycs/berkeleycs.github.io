Spring 2020 CS186 at UC Berkeley

Final

Thursday, May 14, 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM

Logistics

  • Date and Time: Thursday, May 14, 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM
  • Locations: Remote
There will be a 30-minute start window from 11:30am-12pm during which you can start and complete the 3-hour exam.

If you cannot make this start time (conflicting final or timezone difficulties), please email cs186berkeley.issues@gmail.com with your reason for the conflict, the timezone you will be in during the exam date, and a time range (in PDT) within 12 hours of the original final exam start time during you can take the exam. The deadline to submit an alternate request is Wednesday, May 6 at 11:59pm PDT.

Topics

The exam is cumulative.

Preparation

The staff will be hosting review sessions over the weekdays and weekend leading up to the exam.
