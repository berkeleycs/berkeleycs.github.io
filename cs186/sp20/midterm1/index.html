Spring 2020 CS186 at UC Berkeley

Midterm 1

Friday, February 28, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Logistics

  • Date and Time: Friday, February 28, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Locations: This is based on your OFFICIAL section enrollment. Please see the pinned Piazza post "Midterm 1 Room Assignments".
  • 1 sheet (front and back) of hand written notes is allowed.
  • Bring your Cal ID.
  • No calculators are allowed.
  • DSP will receive an email from Professor Hug. Contact the head TAs if you do not receive an email by Monday, February 24.

Topics

The exam will cover Modules 1 - 5.

  • SQL
  • Disks, Buffers, and Files
  • B+ Trees
  • Indices
  • Buffer Management
  • Relational Algebra
  • Sorting & Hashing
