Discussion Schedule

Each discussion will be taught by a TA and have a course tutor present to help out in small groups. To amortize TA/tutor prep time, discussions will be taught by different TAs/tutors in alternating weeks. Some TAs will teach in odd-numbered weeks, while others teach in even-numbered weeks. The week numbers are on the homepage. Discussions start week 1.

Homework Party

Homework Party will be held every Wed, 7-9pm in Cory 540AB. We will have 4-5 course tutors present at HW party to provide support and help you work through the problems. We encourage you to use homework party to work with classmates, find study groups, and get help from course staff on the homework. If you do attend HW party, please mention it when you credit collaborators in your homework submission.
